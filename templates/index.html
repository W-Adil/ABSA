What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment analysis is a natural language processing (NLP) technique that determines whether data is positive, negative, or neutral. It helps businesses:

  • ✓ Monitor brand reputation
  • ✓ Process customer feedback at scale
  • ✓ Identify customer pain points
  • ✓ Improve products and services
Example Analysis

"The product exceeded my expectations!" → Positive (95%)

"Delivery was delayed and item was damaged." → Negative (88%)

"It works as advertised." → Neutral (75%)
